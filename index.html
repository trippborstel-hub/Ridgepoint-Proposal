Carbon Baseline & Materiality

Weeks 2-4

Build GHG Protocol-aligned emissions baselines for each priority company. Full Scope 1 and 2 inventories. Screening-level Scope 3 to identify material categories for deeper analysis.

PHASE 2 DETAIL

Carbon Baseline & Materiality

Full Scope 1 & 2 inventories. Screening-level Scope 3. Materiality assessment mapped to financial outcomes, not generic ESG taxonomies.

What We Do

Calculate emissions baselines using GHG Protocol methodology. Full Scope 1 (direct) and Scope 2 (purchased energy) inventories for all three primary portcos.

Screening-level Scope 3 assessment using spend-based methodology to identify material categories by sector — supply chain for Sterling, employee commuting for Patriot — flagging categories for deeper quantification in a follow-on phase.

Materiality assessment for each company mapping ESG topics to financial drivers: revenue risk, cost exposure, regulatory liability, and buyer/LP expectations.

Why This Matters for PE

The materiality assessment tells you where ESG investment moves the needle on valuation — and where it doesn't. This is what separates an ESG report from a value creation tool.

DELIVERABLES
  • Emissions baseline per portco (Scope 1 & 2 full)
  • Scope 3 screening assessment by category
  • Portfolio-level emissions summary for fund reporting
  • Materiality matrix per portco — ranked by financial impact
  • Methodology documentation (reusable for future updates)
DATA WE NEED FROM YOU

  • Headcount and office square footage by location
